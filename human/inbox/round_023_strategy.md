# Round 23 Strategy Directive

Use the Round 22 judge synthesis together with `rounds/kkt-main/round_022/judge/strategy.md` as the controlling direction for Round 23.

Round 23 is an artifact-completion round, not another architecture round. The main objective is:

$$
\boxed{
\text{Complete the } n=1 \text{ residual endpoint theorem and freeze the lemma bank.}
}
$$

Agent allocation:

- A1: synthesize and close the $n=1$ residual endpoint theorem, or identify the exact remaining scalar obstruction.
- A2: produce one rational-Bessel Volterra lemma with explicit constants, or give a bounded failure with exact obstruction.
- A3: freeze the endpoint-cap and rational-coordinate algebra into lemma-bank form, and audit the Langer residual convention.
- A4: produce actual certified enclosures for the $n=1$ scalar envelope and begin a genuine $n=2$ interval certificate.

Do not allow broad narrative claims to replace theorem statements, constants, or reproducible certificates.
