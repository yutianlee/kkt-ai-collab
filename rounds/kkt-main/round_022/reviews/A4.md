## Certification label legend

* `[PROVED]`: The claim is supported by a complete, rigorous mathematical derivation with no hidden assumptions, missing constants, or unverified asymptotic limits.
* `[DERIVED-UNDER-ASSUMPTIONS]`: The claim is mathematically sound but relies on an explicit, named external theorem or a pending absolute constant that has not yet been numerically or analytically bounded.

* `[LIKELY-FALSE]`: The claim contains a structural flaw, a hidden regime change, or an asymptotic extrapolation that is mathematically invalid under the stated hypotheses.
