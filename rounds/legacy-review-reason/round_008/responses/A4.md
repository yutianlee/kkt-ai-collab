### Step 1: Uniform Bessel Bound

We use the following **rigorous** uniform bound for Bessel functions (Landau 2000, improved by Krasikov 2006):

**Theorem (Krasikov).** For all $\alpha > 0$ and $x > 0$:
$J_\alpha(x) \le \frac{b_0}{(\alpha^2 + x^2)^{1/4}} \cdot \frac{1}{\alpha^{\alpha} e^{-\alpha}} \cdot \frac{(x/2)^\alpha}{\Gamma(\alpha+1)}$

$|J_\alpha(x)| \le \frac{c}{\alpha^{1/3}}, \qquad c = \frac{2^{1/3}}{3^{2/3}\Gamma(2/3)} \approx 0.6749$

This is a classical result; see e.g. Landau (2000) or Olver's bounds.
